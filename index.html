Tomistoma

Tomistoma schlegelii



The false gharial (Tomistoma schlegelii), also known as the Malayan gharial, the Sunda gharial, and by the genus name Tomistoma is a freshwater crocodilian in the family Gavialidae. It is native to Peninsular Malaysia, Borneo, Sumatra, and Java. It is listed as Vulnerable on the IUCN Red List, as the global population is estimated at around 2,500 to 10,000 mature individuals.
